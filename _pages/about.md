I am a Research Assistant at North Dakota State University, working on [USDA](https://www.ars.usda.gov/)-sponsored projects in precision agriculture with machine learning and robotics under the supervision of Dr. James Y. Kim. My current focus is developing software for weed control on an autonomous agriculture vehicle using computer vision and remote sensing. 

Before this role, I received my MSc in Machine Learning and Data Science from Northwestern University and BS in Data Science from the University of California, Irvine.

**Research Interests:**
- Efficient Machine Learning
- Edge Computing
- Precision Agriculture
- Robotics
